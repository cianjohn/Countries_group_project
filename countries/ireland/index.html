Welcome to the Emerald isle,

The island of Ireland, off the west coast of the united kingdom and open to the wild weathers of the Atlantic! it is made up of four provinces Ulster, Leinster, Munster, and Connaught in which are 52 counties, it has a relatively small population of only 4.9 Million.

Things to visit

Fenore

A beautiful beach on the west coast, in county Clare, Munster.

Giants Causeway

A fantastic natural phenominon in county Antrim, Connaught. It is made up of some 40,000 massive black basalt columns sticking out of the sea.

Lough Derg

A massive lake bordering 4 counties: Galway; Clare; Limerick; and tipperary.

The Blarney Stone

Is one of the oldest and classic tourist attractions on the Emerald Isle. According to the old Irish legend, kissing the stone endows the kisser with the ‘gift of the gab’ (great eloquence or skill at flattery).
